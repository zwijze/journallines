Commercial Support and Training

Commercial support and training for JiBX is provided through the companies of project founder Dennis Sosnoski, Sosnoski Software Solutions, Inc. (for clients in the United States) and Sosnoski Software Associates Ltd (for clients worldwide). Beyond the specific classes listed on the web sites for these companies, Dennis is available for facilitating custom in-house classes relating to JiBX at any level of sophistication, from the basics to the most advanced, as well as consulting and custom software development. Email Dennis Sosnoski to find out about rates and scheduling for JiBX-related support and training.

Free Support

Free support is available through the JiBX email lists. Both JiBX developers and other JiBX users will often respond to emails on the lists, though there is no guarantee of a response to any particular issue. Also see the other pages in this section for frequently asked questions and bug reporting information.
